

# JapLan — Sri Lankan Travel Agency Website

A stunning, high-conversion travel website targeting the Japanese market with a majestic, heritage-inspired design.

---

## 1. Visual Foundation & Theme Setup
- **Color palette:** Deep Terracotta, Lush Jungle Green, Antique Gold, with warm neutrals
- **Typography:** Playfair Display for headings (heritage feel), Inter for body text
- **Custom CSS:** Parallax scrolling, glassmorphism effects, ancient Sinhalese-inspired background textures
- **Framer Motion** added for smooth scroll-triggered animations throughout

---

## 2. Hero Section
- Full-width Sigiriya Rock Fortress background (golden hour imagery from Unsplash)
- Headline: "Experience the Legend of Sri Lanka" with optional Japanese subtitle
- Subheadline: "Curated cultural journeys by JapLan"
- Prominent "Plan Your Custom Tour" CTA button with gold accent
- Subtle parallax scroll effect on the background image

---

## 3. "History Comes Alive" — Ancient Sites Grid
- Visual card grid featuring Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, and Polonnaruwa
- Interactive tilt/hover effects on each card
- Fade-in animations as cards enter viewport
- Brief descriptions of each historical site

---

## 4. Custom Tour Builder (Multi-Step Wizard)
- Glassmorphism container floating over a scenic background
- **Step 1 — Duration:** Slider for number of days
- **Step 2 — Vehicle:** Icon cards for Sedan, Luxury Van, Mini Bus
- **Step 3 — Accommodation:** Selectable tiers (Heritage Boutique, Luxury 5-Star, Comfort 3-Star)
- **Step 4 — Interests:** Checkboxes for Ancient History, Wildlife, Beach, Culinary
- **Sticky Summary Panel:** Desktop right-side / mobile bottom — live-updating itinerary summary
- All selections are client-side only (no backend needed for this phase)

---

## 5. Testimonials Section
- Masonry-style grid of review cards
- Each card: traveler photo, star rating, review snippet, "Verified Trip" badge
- Scroll-triggered entrance animations
- Mock data for 6-8 compelling reviews

---

## 6. Footer
- Clean layout with About, Contact, and social media links
- "Specialists in Japan-Sri Lanka Travel" trust badge
- Earthy color scheme consistent with the rest of the site

---

## 7. Global Features
- Fully mobile-responsive across all sections
- Floating WhatsApp button (bottom-right corner)
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Parallax background textures with subtle Sinhalese motifs
